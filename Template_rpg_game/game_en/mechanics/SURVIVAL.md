## 2) Effects of Deprivation

### Hungry (Missed 1 Meal)
- Penalty: -1 to all checks
- Duration: Until food is eaten

### Starving (Missed 1+ Days)
- Penalty: -1 to all checks per day
- After 3+ days: Exhaustion level 1
- After 7+ days: Exhaustion level 2

### Dehydrated (Missed Water)
- First day: -2 to all checks
- Second day: -4 to all checks, exhaustion level 1
- Third day: Unconscious, then death

### Sleep Deprivation
| Hours Missed | Effect |
|--------------|--------|
| 16-24 | Disadvantage on Perception |
| 24-48 | Exhaustion level 1, disadvantage on checks |
| 48+ | Exhaustion level 2, cannot regain HP naturally |

## 3) Environmental Hazards

### Cold Weather
| Temperature | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| 0°C to 10°C | Disadvantage on Survival checks |
| -10°C to 0°C | -1 to checks, need warm clothing |
| Below -10°C | -2 to checks, risk hypothermia |
| Below -20°C | -3 to checks, hourly Constitution save |

### Hot Weather
| Temperature | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| 30°C to 40°C | Need extra water (double) |
| Above 40°C | -1 to checks, risk heat exhaustion |
| Above 50°C | -2 to checks, risk heat stroke |

### Rain and Storms
- Movement halved on rough terrain
- -2 to Perception (hearing obscured)
- Ranged attacks at disadvantage

### Darkness
- Without light source: Blinded condition
- Dim light: Disadvantage on Perception
- torch: 20ft radius bright light, 20ft dim

## 4) Navigation

### Navigation Checks
```
Survival check to navigate
DC = 10 for familiar territory
DC = 15 for wilderness
DC = 20 for trackless terrain
```

### Getting Lost
| Check Result | Effect |
|--------------|--------|
| Fail by 5+ | Wrong direction, lose 1 hour |
| Fail by 10+ | Significantly wrong direction, lose half day |

### Navigation Tools
| Tool | Bonus |
|------|-------|
| Compass | +2 to navigation checks |
| Map | +2 to navigation checks (if accurate) |
| Astrolabe | +5 to navigation (clear sky) |

## 5) Foraging

### Foraging Check
```
Survival check to find food
DC = 10 for abundant area
DC = 15 for normal area
DC = 20 for scarce area
```

### Foraging Results
| Check Result | Food Found |
|--------------|------------|
| Success by 5+ | Full day's food for 2 people |
| Success | Full day's food for 1 person |
| Fail by 5+ | Nothing found, may waste time |

### Hunting
- Requires weapons and hunting skill
- Track check to find prey
- Attack roll to catch/kill
- Larger game provides more food

---

## 6) Rest and Recovery

### Short Rest
- Duration: 1 hour
- Can spend Hit Dice to heal
- Benefits: Recover some stamina

### Long Rest
- Duration: 8 hours (6 must be sleep)
- Recover all lost HP
- Regain all expended Hit Dice
- Can only long rest once per 24 hours

### In Unsafe Locations
- Cannot gain benefits of rest
- Must post guards
- May be interrupted

## 8) Tracking and Trails

### Tracking Check
```
Survival check to track
DC = 10 for obvious trail (many creatures)
DC = 15 for clear trail (few creatures)
DC = 20 for vague trail (one creature)
DC = 25 for very old or careful trail
```

### Tracking Modifiers
| Condition | Modifier |
|-----------|----------|
| Soft ground (mud, snow) | +5 |
| Hard ground (stone) | -5 |
| Rain within last hour | +5 |
| Heavy rain recently | -5 |
| Darkness | -5 |
