## 0) Dice System

### Basic Roll Formula
- **Attribute Check**: `1d20 + Attribute Modifier` vs DC
- **Skill Check**: `1d20 + Skill Bonus` vs DC
- **Attack Roll**: `1d20 + Attack Bonus` vs AC
- **Damage Roll**: `Weapon Die + Strength/Dexterity Modifier`

### Attribute Modifiers
| Score | Modifier |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | -5 |
| 2-3 | -4 |
| 4-5 | -3 |
| 6-7 | -2 |
| 8-9 | -1 |
| 10-11 | +0 |
| 12-13 | +1 |
| 14-15 | +2 |
| 16-17 | +3 |
| 18-19 | +4 |
| 20 | +5 |

## 2) Combat Initiative

### Initiative Formula
```
1d20 + Dexterity Modifier
```

### Initiative Order
1. Roll initiative at combat start
2. Highest roll goes first
3. On ties, highest Dexterity goes first
4. DM may adjust for surprise

| Type | Speed |
|------|-------|

## 5) Rest Rules

### Short Rest
- Duration: 1 hour
- Recovery: 1d6 HP per HD spent
- Can use HD to heal

### Long Rest
- Duration: 8 hours (must include 6 hours of sleep)
- Recovery: Full HP
- Regain all expended HD
- Can only long rest once per 24 hours

### Complete Rest
- Duration: 24 hours
- Recovery: Full HP + remove all status conditions
- Must be in safe location

## 8) Equipment and Encumbrance

### Carrying Capacity
- Light load: Strength score × 5 pounds
- Medium load: Strength score × 10 pounds
- Heavy load: Strength score × 15 pounds

### Encumbrance Penalties
- Light: No penalty
- Medium: -10 ft movement
- Heavy: -20 ft movement, -1 to checks

---

## 9) Optional Rules

### Critical Hits
- Natural 20 on attack roll
- Roll damage dice twice
- Apply to weapon damage only

### Critical Failures
- Natural 1 on attack roll
- Weapon may break (DM discretion)
- Or: lose next action

### Inspiration
- Award for excellent roleplay
- Use for automatic success on one roll
- Or: add +5 to any roll
